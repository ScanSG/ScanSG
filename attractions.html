Singapore has world-class paid attractions and hidden free gems. Smart travelers use both. We’ve split each region into Paid and Free so you can plan like a pro.

Pro Tip: Book paid attractions online for discounts. Free spots? Just show up!

🌆 Central Singapore

Paid Attractions

Marina Bay Sands SkyPark Paid

Iconic infinity pool and panoramic city views. Access only for hotel guests or ticket holders.

💡 Cheat: Book sunset slots via Klook — cheaper than walk-in!

Gardens by the Bay (Cloud Forest & Flower Dome) Paid

Supertrees, futuristic domes, and floral wonders.

💡 Cheat: Visit Supertree Grove for FREE at night. Only domes require tickets.

Universal Studios Singapore (USS) Paid

Thrilling rides and themed zones on Sentosa Island.

💡 Cheat: Buy combo tickets with Sentosa Pass for 15% off!

S.E.A. Aquarium Paid

One of the world’s largest aquariums.

💡 Cheat: Book via Pelago for free cancellation & bundled deals.

National Gallery Singapore Paid

World-class Southeast Asian art.

💡 Cheat: Free entry on Fridays 7–9 PM!

Free Attractions

Marina Bay Light Show Free

Daily light & sound show at 8 PM & 9 PM. Best viewed from Bayfront.

Supertree Grove (Night Lights) Free

Free light show at 7:45 PM & 8:45 PM. Skip the OCBC Skyway if on a budget.

Esplanade – Theatres on the Bay Free

Free performances, rooftop views, and cultural events.

Fort Canning Park Free

Historic green space with trails and ruins.

Chinatown, Little India & Haji Lane Free

Walk through vibrant streets, temples, and boutiques.

🏖️ East Singapore

Paid Attractions

Canopy Park @ Jewel Changi Paid

Topiaries, mazes, and bouncing nets. Separate from the free Rain Vortex.

💡 Cheat: Kids under 3 enter free. Visit on weekdays!

Pulau Ubin Cycling Tour Paid

Rent bikes and explore the island.

💡 Cheat: Rent bikes locally for cheaper than tour packages.

Free Attractions

Rain Vortex @ Jewel Changi Free

World’s tallest indoor waterfall. Open to public 24/7.

East Coast Park Free

Perfect for cycling, BBQs, and beach picnics. Bike rentals available.

Changi Beach Park Free

Quiet beach with views of cargo ships.

Cat Island (Pulau Ubin) Free

Friendly cats near the jetty. Bring treats!

Punggol Waterway Park Free

Scenic boardwalks and family trails.

🎢 West Singapore

Paid Attractions

Night Safari Paid

World’s first nocturnal zoo. Tram ride is a must!

💡 Cheat: Buy Mandai All-Access Pass for 3 parks in one!

Singapore Zoo Paid

Top-rated zoo with open enclosures.

💡 Cheat: NTUC members get 20% off!

River Safari Paid

Home to giant pandas and river animals.

💡 Cheat: Combo tickets save 15% vs buying separately.

Science Centre Singapore Paid

Hands-on exhibits for kids and science lovers.

💡 Cheat: Free entry on your birthday (register online).

Free Attractions

West Coast Park Free

Family-friendly with playgrounds and cycling paths.

NTU Campus Free

Beautiful architecture and art installations.

Boat Quay & Robertson Quay Free

Riverside walks with great sunset views.

🌳 North Singapore

Paid Attractions

Mandai Wildlife Reserve All-Access Pass Paid

Entry to Zoo, Night Safari, River Safari & Bird Paradise.

💡 Cheat: Buy online for 10% off gate price.

Mount Faber Ropeway Paid

Scenic cable car ride with city views.

💡 Cheat: Use an EZ-Link card for 20% off tourist pricing.

Changi Chapel & Museum Paid

Poignant WWII history and stories of POWs.

💡 Cheat: Free for Singaporeans & PRs.

Free Attractions

Bukit Timah Nature Reserve Free

Hike Singapore’s highest point. Lush rainforest.

Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve Free

Mangroves, mudflats & migratory birds.

Yishun Rainbow Playground Free

Colorful, Instagram-famous playground.

Sembawang Hot Spring Park Free

Cook eggs in the natural hot spring!

Thomson Nature Park Free

Abandoned rubber plantation with wildlife.

Kranji War Memorial Free

Poignant tribute to WWII soldiers.

✨ Hidden Gems & Local Favorites

Tiong Bahru Bakery Paid

Famous for kaya croissants. Great coffee too.

💡 Cheat: Go at 7 AM for fresh batch — no queue!

Haw Par Villa Free

Bizarre, colorful statues of Chinese folklore.

💡 Cheat: 100% free. Visit during Chinese festivals for extra vibes.

MacRitchie Reservoir Free

Canopy walk and jogging trails. Otters sometimes appear!

Bishan-Ang Mo Kio Park Free

Beautiful riverside park with playgrounds.

Geylang Serai Market Free

Malay culture, textiles, and food. Best during Ramadan.

Labrador Nature Reserve Free

Coastal trails and WWII bunkers.

Old Hill Street Police Station Free

Colorful heritage building with art galleries.
